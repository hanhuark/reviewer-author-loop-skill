# Reviewer-Author Loop

Use `reviewer-author-loop` to improve a manuscript through repeated review, revision, verification, and re-review.

Workflow:

1. Identify the manuscript source, target venue, and current stage.
2. Review the manuscript with a skeptical but constructive reviewer stance.
3. Convert reviewer comments into traceable author actions.
4. Revise the manuscript directly when source files are editable.
5. Verify build/render, citations, figures, equations, and claim consistency.
6. Re-review the revised manuscript against the previous acceptance criteria.
7. Continue until acceptable, or pause when new evidence, data, experiments, theory, permissions, or author judgment is required.

Expected output:

- reviewer recommendation
- required revisions
- author actions taken
- verification results
- remaining risks
- final stop/pause decision
